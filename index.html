Students too often sacrifice good health for good grades. Our day planning app helps students balance their assignments without sacrificing sleep, while giving teachers the anonymized data they need to prevent burnout.

Our Philosophy:

We believe that wellness and productivity are inseparable.

Students and young professionals have trouble balancing school work, life, and wellness. As a result, college campuses are witnessing a steady decrease in the quality of students' mental health. We believe our holistic approach to productivity can change the culture of work to be more considerate of wellness for students and young professionals.
